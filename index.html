About me

I graduated high school in the summer of 2015 not really sure what career I would like to pursue. I had a couple interviews for some jobs I thought I would fit me. Computers have always been something I've loved but I never thought about coding. Once I found Eleven Fifty I knew it was the right fit for me. I took the 9 week Accelerated Learning Program (ALPs) and it was such an immersive experience. In those 9 weeks we learned HTML5, CSS, JavaScript, and C# and scratched the surface of jQuery. We also worked in Visual Studio 2015, Sublime Text, and worked with Azure. We also learned the basics of Git source control.
